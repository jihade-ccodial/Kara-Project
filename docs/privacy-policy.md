# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2025

## 1. Introduction

Kara ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our sales team management and coaching platform ("Service").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password, and profile information
- **Team Data**: Team names, member assignments, and organizational structure
- **Goals and Metrics**: Sales goals, targets, and performance metrics you create
- **Meeting Data**: 1-on-1 meeting notes, todos, and scheduling information

### 2.2 HubSpot Data
When you connect your HubSpot account, we access:
- **Deals**: Deal names, amounts, stages, close dates, and properties
- **Pipelines**: Pipeline configurations and stage definitions
- **Owners**: Team member names, emails, and HubSpot IDs
- **Engagements**: Calls, emails, and meetings associated with deals
- **Deal Properties**: Custom properties and metadata

**Note**: We only access data necessary for the Service's functionality as specified in our OAuth scopes.

### 2.3 Google Calendar Data
When you connect your Google Calendar, we access:
- **Calendar Events**: Event titles, descriptions, dates, times, and attendees
- **Calendar Metadata**: Calendar IDs and timezone information

**Note**: We use this data solely to identify and manage 1-on-1 meetings.

### 2.4 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the Service, features used, and time spent
- **Device Information**: Browser type, device type, operating system, and IP address
- **Log Data**: Server logs, error reports, and performance metrics
- **Cookies**: Session cookies and authentication tokens

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption**: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- **Authentication**: Secure OAuth authentication with HubSpot and Google
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to data on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Audits**: Security audits and vulnerability assessments
- **Token Management**: Secure storage and rotation of authentication tokens

### 5.2 Data Breach Procedures
In the event of a data breach:
- We will notify affected users promptly
- We will report breaches to relevant authorities as required by law
- We will take immediate steps to mitigate the breach

### 5.3 Your Responsibilities
- Keep your account credentials secure
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Log out when using shared devices
- Notify us immediately of any unauthorized access
